The permanent residents

Nearly 600 cemeteries and burial grounds across the five boroughs — the active, the preserved and the long-paved-over — with burial counts and notable residents where they're documented.
Methodology

About this page

  • This map was built with AI assistance. Active-cemetery shapes come from OpenStreetMap; the fuller inventory — including historic and obliterated burial grounds, and the founding years and status labels — comes from Elizabeth D. Meade's Cemeteries of New York City catalogue. Notable burials come from Wikidata.
  • Burial counts were checked one by one against cemetery websites, Wikipedia article text and news sources, with a source link and confidence level shown for each. Most cemeteries publish no figure at all.
  • "Notable burials" means people with Wikipedia/Wikidata entries who are recorded there as buried at the cemetery — an incomplete and uneven list.
  • Spot-check anything you plan to rely on. See the methodology for details.
